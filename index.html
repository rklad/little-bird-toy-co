About Us

Founded on October 24, 2018, we started our small, family-owned business with a standard of excellence. We started out in Marlboro, New Jersey, making the very best wooden toys in the town. Our three founders, Evan, Abigail, and Elizabeth Thorne founded the company, personally making every toy sold. Over time, they grew their family name around the state of New Jersey and became the three CEOs. Evan runs the advertising component of the company, Abigail is in charge of the business decisions of the company, and Elizabeth works on the toys.

From the start, our sales drastically exceeded our expectations and we were able to expand a lot sooner than expected. We opened up two more locations in New Jersey, one in Montclair and the other in Cherry Hill. For the past 6 years, we have upheld our initial standards of excellence by creating over 100 unique and original wooden toys for children. Our hopes for the future are to maintain this standard, continue developing our own unique wooden toys, and to continue expanding to new locations, maybe even out of state.
